Privacy Policy

GTKR Video respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

  • Video uploads and metadata (title, video file, duration)
  • Usage data such as IP address and browser type

How We Use Information

  • To display and manage video content

  • To communicate with users if needed

Data Protection

All user data and video uploads are stored securely using Cloudinary and MongoDB. We do not share your personal data with third parties without consent.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updated policies will be posted on this page.
